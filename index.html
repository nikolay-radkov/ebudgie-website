How it works?

Manage your money in one place. See what is going on with your budget at any time and at any place.

  • Customize your categories and items
  • Regularly update your expenses and incomes
  • Set your profile settings
  • Add monthly threshold and be updated for its status as soon as possible
  • See the results at the end of month
  • Export your data

Our story

There are a lot of apps for budget managing but each of them has some missing parts. In this app, we wanted to include every useful feature for similar apps and make it easier to use.

Our Vision

Making people's lives less hard by developing our product is our main goal. The idea of offline first app, app that doesn't need an internet to run is one of the key factors for building our infrastructure.

Technology

We are using cutting edge technologies and trying to include as more useful features as possible.
